Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 26, 2026

Echo Path is an offline single-player sonar exploration puzzle. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Information Collection

Echo Path does not collect personal information. The app does not include account registration, social login, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party purchase services.

Local Game Data

The app stores gameplay progress and unlocked levels locally on your device using NSUserDefaults. This information stays on your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

Echo Path is suitable for all ages and rated 4+. Because the app does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

Internet, Ads, and Purchases

  • No internet connection is required for gameplay.
  • No advertisements are included.
  • No in-app purchases are included.
  • No user tracking is included.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with a new effective date.

Contact

After release, you can contact support through the Echo Path App Store product page or visit the support site.
